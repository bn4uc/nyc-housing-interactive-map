Visualizing HMDA Data in New York City

By Brianna Noonan, 3/19

Homeownership is a crucial contributor to wealth, stability, and opportunity. The Home Mortgage Disclosure Act Data includes characteristics of mortgage applications, outcomes, and applicant demographic information. This map displays selected variables for New York City by Neighborhood Tabulation Area (NTA) for different races and years.

Hover over neighborhoods and use buttons and dropdown menu to explore!
